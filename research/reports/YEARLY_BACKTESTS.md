## How to read this report

1. **5-year compound** is what $10,000 would have grown to over 2021-2026
2. **Annual %** is the geometric mean growth rate
3. **Adversarial Verdict** is the deploy gate from `research/ai/adversarial_validator.py`:
   - **PASS** = all 3 stress windows positive
   - **WARN** = 1 window negative, worst > -15%
   - **FAIL** = 2+ negatives or worst > -15%
   - **CATASTROPHIC** = worst < -30%

The deploy decision was:
- PASS → full wallet ($3-5K)
- WARN → reduced wallet ($2-3K)
- FAIL/CATASTROPHIC → NO DEPLOY
